Welcome to Oklahoma!

Welcome to Oklahoma! We’re so glad you have decided to relocate to the OK state. We’ve provided some information here on some of the top destinations of soon to be Oklahomans. We’re also excited to tell you a bit about what makes us special.

Oklahoma is the perfect place for those looking for adventure. With exciting weather, a host of wildlife ranging from black bears to alligators, and lots of open spaces for you to explore, Oklahoma has plenty of excitement available for you.

Fun Facts

  • At the heart of tornado alley, Oklahoma typically sees an average of 62 tornadoes per year.
  • Oklahoma has lots of natural spaces, with 51 state parks, 6 national parks or protected areas, and 2 national protected areas.
  • Two-thirds of Oklahomans live within the Oklahoma City and Tulsa metropolitan areas.
