Oklahoma City

Oklahoma city is the capital of Oklahoma. A thriving urban city, it is the 20th largest city in the United States and the 8th largest city in the south. Oklahoma city is home to one of the largest livestock markets in the world.

One of the largest employers in Oklahoma City is the Tinker Air Force Base, which employs around 26,000 people. Other major employers include Amazon, Hobby Lobby Stores, and Devon Energy.

Oklahoma City also has several attractions including the Myriad Botanical Gardens, the Museum of Osteology, and the American Banjo Museum. There are also many parks and pathways scattered throughout the city.
